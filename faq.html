  • Bob1 is a very difficult course in Bob International School. It is recommended to take the prerequisite course 'Bob-Start' to strengthen your foundations in 'Bob'.
  • How to take a leave of absence?
  • To take a leave of absence, first go to the Bob Support Office (BSO) and talk the the staff over there. Then beg to them saying you need to take a leave of absence (note that begging has a 30% chance of succeeding).
  • Why can't I take the Bob Exams?
  • If you are unable to take the Bob Exams, then that means your attendence for that particular Bob course is lower than 80%. To take the Bob Finals for any particular course your attendence for that course must be at least 80%
